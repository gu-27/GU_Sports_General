Where we go from here
Two ideas that could
define the next chapter.
Proposed resource
Mental Fitness Lab
A dedicated space where every Gonzaga student can develop the mental skills that sport demands — and life requires.
  • Visualization, mindfulness, and focus as daily practice — not crisis intervention
  • Peer-led cohorts rooted in Ignatian reflection and the daily examen
  • Open to every student — no prior athletic background required
  • Connects the Mind and Spirituality pillars of the formation framework into a single place
  • Distinct from counseling services — this is performance and formation, not treatment
Proposed program · Modeled on Hogan Entrepreneurship
Sports Identity Cohort
A selective, cross-disciplinary cohort for students who want to go deep — applying their major to sports as a career path and formation journey.
  • Competitive application — open to students in any school (SBA, Engineering, Education, Health Sciences, Law)
  • Cohort moves together across two years — peer accountability, shared identity, and deep formation
  • Experiential learning at the core: real consulting engagements, practicum placements, and applied research in sports
  • Faculty from multiple schools co-facilitate — sports becomes the integrating theme across disciplines
  • Like Hogan for entrepreneurship: a community signal that Gonzaga takes this seriously

"These are not additions to what Gonzaga already does. They are the institutional structures that make sports identity a first-class part of what it means to be a Zag."
